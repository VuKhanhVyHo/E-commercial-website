•••CANCELLATION AND REFUND•••

The payment will be made based on your type of arts:

+ Line art:

- If you pre-paid 100%, you will receive a 100% refund before I finish the sketch.

Otherwise, you will get 50% refund.

- If you chose to pay after I finish the art. Then you can cancel before I start your commission. Otherwise, you will have to pay 70% the price for me if I finished the sketch or 40% if I didn’t.

+ Colour:

- If you pre-paid 100%, you will receive a 100% refund before I finish the sketch, 80% refund If I finished the sketch, 65% if I have done the line art, 30% if I just colour some details and 25% if I nearly done the art

- Otherwise, You will have to pay 50% after I finished the line sketch and rest after I finished the colour step. You can cancel if I haven’t started the comm yet. Otherwise, you will have to pay 25% if I finished the sketch, 50% if I finish the line art, 75% if I just colour some details and 80% if I nearly done the art.
